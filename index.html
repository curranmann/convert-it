What is a Valid Lead?

At RLC Agency, we consider a lead valid when it meets the following criteria:

  • The caller is a homeowner (not a renter or property manager)
  • The caller is seeking HVAC repair or installation/replacement services
  • The caller is located within your defined service area
  • The call lasts a minimum of 60 seconds or results in a booked appointment
  • The call is not a duplicate from the same customer within a 30-day period

When Payment is Due

Payment is only due for calls that meet our valid lead criteria. You will never pay for:

  • Wrong numbers or spam calls
  • Solicitation calls
  • Calls from outside your service area
  • Calls about services you don't offer
  • Duplicate calls from the same customer within 30 days
